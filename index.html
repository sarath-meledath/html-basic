About the Fest

Aurora Fest 2026 brings together students from every department for three days of competitions, workshops, and performances. This year's theme is "Innovate. Create. Celebrate."

Events

Techathon

Date: September 12, 2026

A 12-hour overnight hackathon where teams of up to 4 build a working prototype around a surprise problem statement announced at the start.

Battle of Bands

Date: September 13, 2026

College bands compete live on the main stage for a chance to open for the closing-night headline act.

Painting Palette Workshop

Date: September 14, 2026

A hands-on acrylic painting workshop for beginners, led by the Fine Arts department. All materials are provided.
